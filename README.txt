Berytus is a browser-based password manager governance framework 
for programmable authentication and registration sessions.

Head over to the proof of concept website to start experimenting 
with Berytus:

    https://poc.berytus.io

Berytus is detailed in a research paper that was published in the
proceedings of the 2024 International Conference on Information 
and Communications Security. The ePrint is available on arXiv:

    https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.07205
